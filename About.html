About us

EIMC is one of the leading industrial real estate and industrial sectors merger &acquisition consulting firm in Vietnam.

Our headquarter is based in Ho Chi Minh City, a very dynamic and largest city of Vietnam. We provide the best solution and perfect service in industrial real estate investment, development, management, brokerage through our exclusive management, consulting expertise and additional relevant services for manufacturers or industrial investors when they have an intend, plan to invest or expand their manufacturing business in Viet Nam . In addition, to investment edge as our advanced service, we provide the merger and acquisition consulting service in industrial manufacturing sectors for the SME manufacturing companies, industrial investor as a featured & advanced consulting service .

In industrial estate sector, we have a large industrial estate portfolio with diversified products such as industrial land, factories and warehouse in a wide range of business including of leasing, selling, build to suite for lease or sale, sell and lease back. We could be a representative for owner, landlord, investor to manage the industrial estates, projects to attract investors, to call investment into or to sell or lease out, to looking for strategic partner. In large scale, we are very proud to be chief representative of many prestigious industrial estate investors and developers in Viet Nam (almost of them located in Southern Key Economic Region [SKER]), several of them in the central and the north region such as Becamex Corp (Binh Duong), Sonadezi (Dong Nai), Saigon VRG (Ho Chi Minh City), Dai An IP (Hai Duong),...

We provide the complete solution for whole process of industrial investment, development, running business from the beginning to final step by extensive solution and premier consulting with a high-serving spirit and detail-looking to bring the most effectiveness. We are not only providing simply industrial space, investment opportunities but also important consulting service to getting high ROI in during of investment, development, business such as feasibility study, research and assessment, due-diligence, site selection, doing investment procedure, contracting, leasing & selling, bridge to partner or supplier connection & selection and even additional industrial contractor selection consulting for the demand of building industrial factory.

Our commitment and dedication in providing quality products, best services, and perfect solution for our industrial investors, manufacturers. Those are our core values and our responsibility and we focus highly on it. Our valued customers benefit from our expertise, resource, quality products, good solution, extensive network, and dedicated team with deeply local knowledge, experience.

At EIMC, we always put the value and benefit of customers on the top line and that’s our bottom line, and make sure that they feel the most comfortable, convenient, benefit-able when they work with us.

EIMC is a strategic and totally trusted partner with industrial investors, industrial developers, prestigious companies, relevant partners, international manufacturers, as well as leading domestic manufacturers in Vietnam. We are pride of to be who we are and always try the best to providing the quality products, good solution in the industrial estate sector and industrial manufacturing sectors in Vietnam.
