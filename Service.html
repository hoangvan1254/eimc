Pre-LICENSING

STEPS

Application for Business Registration Certificate

After obtaining Business Registration Certificate, EIMC does check carefully the information stated on Business Registration Certificate before leaving Business Registrar. In case of incorrect information, we immediately contact the official sending the result to fix it in accordance with law

Application for Investment Certificate

* For domestic enterprise, business registration first, then investment registration
* For international enterprise, investment registration first, then business registration

POST-INVESTMENT PROCEDURES

Legal procedures after singing Land Re-lease Contract, step by step as following:

  1. Fire protection appraisal upon technical drawing or blueprint
  2. Register environmental impact evaluation statement
  3. Register environmental protection plan
  4. Deploy construction
  5. Accept and confirm completed construction
  6. Land Use Right Certificate
  7. Fire protection acceptance
  8. Completed construction certificate
  9. Update Assets attached to the land ownership into Land Use Right Certificate
  10. Change content of Business Registration
  11. Register Labor Permit for foreigner employees
  12. Register rules on labor
  13. Register collective labor agreement

* Construction permit: 2-3 weeks
* Labor permit: 2-3 weeks
